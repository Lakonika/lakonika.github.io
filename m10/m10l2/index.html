Read the dialogue

Ann: I’d really like to go abroad this summer.

Bill: Why don’t you go to Egypt?

Ann: Egypt? What can you do there?

Bill: Well, there’s a lot to see. For example, you can take a wonderful cruise on the Nile and see the ancient sites.

Ann: What else can you do?

Bill: You can learn a lot about Egypt’s history and culture.

Ann: Hmm. Egypt sounds like a good idea!
