vocabulary

reading

  • stripe = long line which is a different colour from the area next to it
  • hide = go somewhere that he cannot be easily seen by others
  • hunt = chase and kill wild animals for food
  • dangerous = likely to harm or hurt you
  • head = leader
  • heavy = weighing a lot
  • trunk = the long nose elephants have
  • horn a hard pointed thing that grows out of an animal's head
  • alone = not with anyone else
  • mud = sticky mixture of earth and water

Spelling rules

  • verb + s

    / eat - he eats, I like - he likes

  • verb -ss/-sh/-ch/-x/-o + es

    I go - he goes, / wash - she washes

  • verb ending in a consonant y: -ies

    / cry - he cries but / say - he says
