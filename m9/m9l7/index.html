Sergiev Posad near Moscow is famous for its wooden toys. They still make toys there today, and they’ve got a great Toy Museum.

There are about 30000 toys in the museum. There are old wooden ‘Trinity’ toys with moving parts. There are lots of dolls and matryoshkas and toy soldiers. There are modern electronic toys too.

You must go there some day!
