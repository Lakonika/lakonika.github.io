ex.2 p.112

Read the dialogues. What are these dialogues about? Listen, read and check. Where does each person want to go? Find the places on the map.

Dialogue 1

  • A: Excuse me, is there a fast food near here?
  • B: Yes, there's one on Berry Street.
  • A: Where exactly?
  • B: On the corner of Eldon Road and Berry Street.
  • A: Thanks a lot.

Dialogue 2

  • A: Excuse me, can you tell me where the cinema is?
  • B: Yes, sure. Go down Bath Street and turn right. Walk down Eldon Road. The cinema is on your left.
  • A: On my left?
  • B: Yes. It's opposite the fast food restaurant. You can't miss it.
  • A: Thank you.
