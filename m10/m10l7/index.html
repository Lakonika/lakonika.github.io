Reading

Match the texts to the headings

  • Wild animals are interesting and exciting to see, but they are still wild and dangerous. Tourists and campers sometimes forget about that.
  • Never touch or get too close to a wild animal. Even if they seem nice and cute, they can carry illnesses. They can also attack or bite you! Never get between any wild animal and its young. Animals will always protect their young.
  • Never feed a wild animal. It can be harmful to their health.
  • Follow the rules for food storage. Never leave food in your tent or out on your picnic table: the food will attract wildlife to your campsite.
